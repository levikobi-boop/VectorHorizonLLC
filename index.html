About

Who We Are

Vector Horizon LLC is a solo independent development studio focused on building thoughtful, fun mobile experiences. We design every game from the ground up — from procedural puzzle generation to custom multiplayer infrastructure.

Our goal is simple: make games we'd want to play ourselves. Clean design, fair monetization, and genuine replayability.

Based in the United States, Vector Horizon LLC publishes on both Google Play and the Apple App Store.
