Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 2026  ·  Vector Horizon LLC

This page explains how to delete your account and its associated data for Vector Horizon LLC apps that offer accounts, such as Codebreaker. Apps that do not use accounts store data only on your device, which is removed when you uninstall the app.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (immediate)

In Codebreaker:

  • Open the app and sign in.
  • Go to the Home screen.
  • Tap Delete Account (below Logout) and confirm.

This permanently and immediately deletes your account and all associated data (see below). This action cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you cannot access the app, email us at support@vectorhorizonllc.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject “Account Deletion.” We will verify and process your request.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account removes the following from our systems:

  • Your account details: email address, display name, and user ID.
  • Your game data: scores, ELO rating, win/loss/draw records, daily-puzzle streaks and totals, and unlocked achievements.
  • Your gameplay and matchmaking preferences.
  • Your authentication account.

Data retention

Account data is deleted promptly upon request. We may retain limited information only where required to comply with legal obligations or to prevent fraud and abuse; otherwise your data is removed. Anonymous analytics and advertising data are handled per Google’s policies. Data stored locally on your device is removed when you uninstall the app.

Contact

Questions about account deletion: support@vectorhorizonllc.com
